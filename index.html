About Me

I'm a pretty chill person most of the time, but ask me about things related to video games and anime and I will be on that topic for days. I'm good at keeping these things to myself until I know that the other person has the same interests as me. These things have become major parts in my life and because of that, I would always have the urge to create. Digital drawing is another hobby I like to do to pass the time.
